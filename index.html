About RuBan

The RuBan trans tech is a business setup to commercially invade into the agricultural and logistics sector of the Nigerians economy with technology as its driving force. It is an online application that provides a digital of all kinds of food products to upload their goods to a wide range of consumers and in addition, offers room for increased exportation of agricultural produce in the country.

On the other hand, we get to improve the lifestyle of the population with a 'can't do without commodity' - Food, at its most affordable and healthiest condition. The end result of this goal provides:
